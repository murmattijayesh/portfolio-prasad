## Overview

Soiling on pyranometer and albedometer lenses degrades the accuracy of weather-
and solar-monitoring instruments. As an R&D Design Intern at Aeron Systems, I
designed a prototype robot to automate cleaning of these lenses, keeping readings
reliable with minimal manual intervention.

## Highlights

- Designed the prototype assembly in SolidWorks with detailed part and assembly drawings
- Applied GD&T and managed assembly configurations for production readiness
- Prepared BOMs, MICD and engineering documentation to internal standards
- Built VBA macros in SolidWorks to automate repetitive drawing tasks such as figure-label renumbering across multi-sheet drawings
