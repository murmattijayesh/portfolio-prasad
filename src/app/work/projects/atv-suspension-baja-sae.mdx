## Overview

As Vehicle Dynamics Engineer for Team Conquistador (PVG's COET), I designed the
suspension system for the team's Baja SAE all-terrain vehicle, balancing ride,
handling and durability for rough off-road conditions.

## Highlights

- Designed and modelled the ATV suspension system in SolidWorks
- Validated the design through structural analysis in ANSYS
- Optimised suspension geometry, load distribution and ride–handling balance
- Achieved 1st Runner-Up in Suspension & Traction at the ATVC competition
