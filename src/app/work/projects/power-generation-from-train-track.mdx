## Overview

This project explores harvesting energy from the motion of passing trains. As a
train moves over the track, a piston-based mechanism captures the otherwise-wasted
kinetic energy and converts it into electrical energy.

## Highlights

- Designed a piston-based mechanism to capture motion from passing trains
- Converted kinetic energy into electrical energy via a crankshaft-driven generator
- Demonstrates mechanism design and energy-conversion fundamentals
