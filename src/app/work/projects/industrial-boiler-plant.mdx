As a mechanical design intern at SGM Tech, I contributed to the full design and
development of a boiler manufacturing plant, from initial layout planning through
to system integration.

## Highlights

- Mechanical design and detailing of boiler components, piping layouts and auxiliary systems using CAD tools
- Plant layout, material-flow planning and equipment placement to optimise manufacturing efficiency and safety
- Practical exposure to large-scale industrial mechanical systems and plant integration
